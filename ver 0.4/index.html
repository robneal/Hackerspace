What is the Hackerspace

The HackerSpace is a place where students can learn and experiment with hardware, electronics , and software systems. It is run by the Computer Science department but is open to any Rutgers Student.

Hardware

The hackerspace is home to a lot of really cool hardware, among which include 3-D printers , Robots, Virtual Reality displays, and more. Check out the rest of our hardware in the HackerSpace Library or come by and visit us.

Electronics

We also have a lot of electorics here at the HackersSpace such as Raspberry Pis, Arduinos, Servos , and Motors. To veiw these and other electronics check out the hackerSapce Library or come by for a visit.

Software Systems

The HackerSpace provides access to a host of sofware systems. These include iOS and Android development a Newtwork Attack System and more. To learn about all our sofware system check out the hackerSapce Library or come by for a visit us.
