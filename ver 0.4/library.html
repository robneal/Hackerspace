The HackerSpace Library

Welcome to the HackerSpace Library, here you will find useful documents to get you started using most of the Hardware, Electronics and Software Systems we have. Most of these documents were written by our HackerSpace Mentors and are meant for beginners.

If you have something you want to contribute(please do!) feel free to email mcgrew@cs.rutgers.edu. There will be a hackerspace wiki (there's one for everything else in the universe, why not this?) soonlyish, to make contributing more seemless. (Yes, that's a pun.)
